ABOUT US

PAWSITIVITY is a complete non-profitable organization that focus on rescuing and rehoming those pet which have been abandoned or abused. We provide proper care,shelter,medical to all the animals in need. We have different breeds of dogs along with few cats and other animals. The main goal of our organization is to provide a friendly and peaceful environment to all.

Along with Adoptions , we conduct several campaign and rescuing programs on a regular basis. You can also be a part of this program by filling a short form with your full dedication and also you can help us with donations. Last year, we conducted total 50 campaigns at different places.
